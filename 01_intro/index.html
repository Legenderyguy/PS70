Week 1: Final Project Proposal

Here are at most 3 ideas for my final project:

Idea 1: Modern Man's Wrist Watch

Imagine a wrist watch that shows:

  • Wi-Fi Speed
  • Time
  • Weather

Idea 2: Desktop-Sized Robotic Sweeper

Imagine a little robot controlled by you, sweeping all of the dust from your table so cleaning becomes a fun game.

Idea 3: SleepyBuddy – The Rubber Band Enforcer

Many people struggle to maintain a stable sleep schedule—just like me! The solution? Meet SleepyBuddy! SleepyBuddy keeps you company during deep work sessions, but when bedtime hits, it will do whatever it takes to get you to sleep. At first, it’ll give you friendly warnings. Ignore those? It’ll start shooting rubber bands until you head to bed!
