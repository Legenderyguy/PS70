Version Explanations

Version 1

In this first version of my stage, my goal was to make a quick & undetailed sketch of the scene. You will see that this design has changed a lot in further versions.

Version 2

In the second version, I put holes on the walls of my scene to be able to install the upper floor of the stage.

Version 3

In the third version, I realized that the lines between the holes could be a problem (so I removed them). Also, I added holes into the side walls for the dowel to go through.

Version 4

The holes for the upper floor were too close to the top edge of the wall, which risked cutting errors. I moved them down and added spacing to improve durability.

Version 5

I shortened the backplate to save material. Originally it was a background, but I decided to focus more on the mechanism instead of visual complexity.

Version 6

I realized that the backplate might interfere with working on the internal mechanisms, so I removed it to improve access and simplicity.
