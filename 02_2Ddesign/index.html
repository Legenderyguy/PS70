Week 2: 2D Design & Cutting

The Japanese Pull Saw

Tools I used:
- Caliper: To measure the dimensions of the object.
- Meterstick: To measure dimensions that are too big to measure with the caliper.

Software I used in this design:
- Fusion 360
Main functions I used inside Fusion 360:
- Pattern on Path: For the teeth of the pull saw.
- Fit Point Spline: For making parts that are not fully horizontal/vertical (e.g., the blade).
- Sketch & Extrude: For turning my 2D sketches into 3D objects.

The Mallet

Tools I used:
- Caliper: To measure the dimensions of the object.
- Meterstick: To measure dimensions that are too big to measure with the caliper.

Software I used in this design:
- Fusion 360
Main functions I used inside Fusion 360:
- Fit Point Spline: For making parts that are not fully horizontal/vertical (e.g., the head of the Mullet).
- Rotation: For revolving the sketch of the Mullet's Head 360 degrees to get a 3D cylinder like shape.
